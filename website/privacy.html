Privacy Policy

How we respect your privacy when you visit LofiMellowHQ.studio.

Last updated: 14 July 2026 · Effective for https://lofimellowhq.studio

1. Who we are

LofiMellowHQ (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the official artist website at https://lofimellowhq.studio. We create and distribute original Lo-Fi, neo-classical piano, ambient, sleep, rain, study and focus music.

Contact: hello@lofimellowhq.studio

2. What this site does

This website is a static artist presence hosted on Cloudflare Pages. It presents brand information, discography, streaming links, and an optional contact form. We do not operate user accounts on this site.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

If you use the contact form, you may provide your name, email address, subject and message. On this static deployment, form submission opens your own email client (mailto) so the message is sent from you to us. We receive only what you choose to send by email.

3.2 Technical / hosting data

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may process standard server logs such as IP address, browser type, referrer, and request timestamps for security, performance and reliability. We do not sell this information.

3.3 Cookies

This site does not set first-party advertising cookies. Cloudflare or your browser may use technical cookies necessary for delivery and security. Third-party streaming platforms you visit via our links (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) have their own privacy policies.

4. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries you send us
  • To operate, secure and improve the website
  • To comply with legal obligations when required

5. Legal bases (EEA / UK visitors)

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process personal data on the basis of legitimate interests (running a secure artist website) and/or your consent or contract-related necessity when you contact us voluntarily.

6. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers who host or secure the site (e.g. Cloudflare), or if required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.

7. International transfers

Our audience is worldwide. Hosting and email infrastructure may process data in the United States or other countries. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied by providers under their terms.

8. Retention

Email correspondence is retained only as long as needed to handle your request and maintain ordinary business records, unless a longer period is required by law.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object or lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise rights related to data we hold from your emails, contact hello@lofimellowhq.studio.

10. Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Third-party links

Links to Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube, social networks and other services are provided for convenience. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after updates constitutes awareness of the revised policy.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@lofimellowhq.studio
Or use our contact page.
